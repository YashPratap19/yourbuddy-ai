Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about YourBuddy.ai and how it can support your mental health.

Is YourBuddy.ai a replacement for professional therapy?

No, YourBuddy.ai is not a replacement for professional mental health treatment. While our AI companion can provide support, coping strategies, and helpful resources, it should be viewed as a complementary tool. For serious mental health concerns, we recommend consulting with a licensed mental health professional.

How secure and private are my conversations?

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are encrypted end-to-end, and we do not share your personal data with third parties. You can also delete your conversation history at any time. Our detailed privacy policy outlines how we protect your information.

What mental health issues can YourBuddy.ai help with?

YourBuddy.ai can provide support for common challenges like stress, anxiety, mild depression, sleep issues, and everyday emotional wellbeing. It offers coping strategies, mindfulness exercises, and emotional support for these concerns.

How much does YourBuddy.ai cost?

YourBuddy.ai offers a free basic plan with limited daily interactions. Our premium plan at $9.99/month provides unlimited conversations, advanced coping tools, detailed mood tracking, and personalized insights. We also offer an annual plan with a 20% discount.

Can I use YourBuddy.ai on different devices?

Yes, YourBuddy.ai is available on web, iOS, and Android. Your account and conversation history sync across devices, allowing you to continue your mental health journey seamlessly wherever you are.
